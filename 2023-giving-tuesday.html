Join Edward Johnson School Council today in celebrating #GivingTuesday, a day dedicated to charitable giving.

We know there are so many charities deserving of your support at this time of year. As you plan your year-end giving we hope you will consider making a donation to Edward Johnson Public School(EJPS).

Fundraising for our new playground is well underway, our council is committed to improve the mental and health well-being of students at EJPS. This year in collaboration with our annual Winter Basket giveaway we are reaching out to parents, grandparents and guardians to commit to a small donation of $10 per month.

With the assistance of Canada Helps and the Upper Grand Learning Foundation, EJPS can directly benefit from your donations.

To make your online donation:

  • Click the DONATE button below
  • Select monthly donation
  • Select Edward Johnson PS in the drop down menu
  • Fill in required fields to complete the donation
